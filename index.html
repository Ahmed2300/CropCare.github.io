Easy Diagnosis Input

Getting a diagnosis is effortless. Use your phone's camera to take a clear photo or simply upload an existing image from your gallery.

Our AI is ready to analyze the visual data for signs of diseases or deficiencies.

Scan History & Tracking

Keep a detailed record of all your plant scans. Easily track the health progression of individual plants or crops over time.

Quickly see past diagnoses or confirmations of health. Includes timestamps and detected issues.

AI Analysis In Progress

Sit back while our powerful, trained AI model analyzes the image you provided. The visual scanning process identifies patterns, discoloration, and textures indicative of various conditions.

Processing usually takes just a few moments.

Receive clear, actionable results. When a disease or issue is detected, CropCare provides:

Health Confirmation

Good news! When no issues are detected, CropCare confirms the plant appears healthy based on the visual analysis.

We provide a confidence score reflecting the AI's certainty and a low danger level indicator.

Follow these simple steps to install the CropCare app using the APK file.
